## What data is collected

This site uses a self-hosted analytics tool to count page visits. It records which pages are viewed and where traffic comes from (e.g. a search engine or a link), but it does **not** use cookies, does not fingerprint your browser, and does not track you across sites or sessions. No personally identifiable information is stored.

## Google Fonts

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts. When your browser requests a font file, Google may log your IP address as part of a standard web request. See [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details.

## No accounts, no forms, no cookies

This site does not have user accounts, does not ask for your name or email, and does not set any cookies.
